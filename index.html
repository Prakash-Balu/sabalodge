Welcome to Hotel Sabanayagam

Hotel Sabanayagam is a landmark lodge with modern amenities, and located close to the famous nataraja temple in Chidambaram,Tamil Nadu (0.1km).. We have Ac and Non-Ac rooms to suit your requirements. All of our bedrooms are usually large and designed to create a feeling of space and light. Each room is equipped with the latest facilities, including interactive satellite television. The bus stand, railway station are to be found within striking distance from the hotel Sabanayagam Lodge. We are attuned to the needs of all our clients-tourist and business people and to feel at home, choose to stay in hotel sabanayagam.
